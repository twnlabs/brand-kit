# Brand Usage Guide

## Brand colors

- Primary Purple: `#6B3CFF`
- Accent Aqua: `#00EEC5`
- Pure White: `#FFFFFF`

## Spacing & minimum sizes

- Clear space: at least the height of the letter "T" around all sides.
- Minimum sizes
  - Digital logo: 120px width
  - Print logo: 1 inch (25.4mm) width
  - Icon-only: 32px

## FAQ

- What can I do with TWN Labs brand assets?
  - Use in marketing, press, partnership announcements, and educational content while preserving spacing, contrast, proportions, and quality.
- What should I avoid?
  - No stretch/skew/rotate/distort. Do not change colors or add effects. Avoid complex imagery behind the logo. Do not use outdated or self-made variants.
- Available formats?
  - PNG (raster) and SVG (vector). Use SVG for crisp scaling and print; PNG for digital.
- Do I need permission?
  - Standard use per these guidelines requires no extra permission. For commercial partnerships, merchandise, or modified usage, contact `partners@twnlabs.com`.
- Can I use different colors?
  - Only official variations: full-color on light bg, white on dark, black on light. No custom colors or gradients.
